Hello!

Welcome to my website! My name is Oluebube Ivy Anukam. I am an aspiring (and self-learning) front-end developer on Mondays, Wednesdays, Fridays and Saturdays; aspiring (and also self-learning) game developer on Tuesdays and Fridays and a couch on Sundays. I write on Mondays.

Fun fact: I love writing short stories more because it presents an interesting challenge. I can say most everything I want to say when writing a long piece but the conservation of words that come with short stories means I have to put in only the words most meaningful to the story while still incorporating nuance if I want it to be a good short story.
